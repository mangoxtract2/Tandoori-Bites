Who even are we?

We are two 8th graders teaming up to create a fake food truck website. Since we met in 6th grade, we've developed a strong friendship and tackled many coding challenges together. We couldn't ask for a better partner than each other to start this food truck adventure with. Our shared enthusiasm for coding and love for food will fuel our creativity as we are bringing our virtual food truck to life. With cramped fingers and endless determination, we're excited to continue on this journey together. Let's make our food truck dreams a reality!

Our Story

My best friend Harnoor and I, Nethra, started a food truck called Tandoori Bites to bring Indian flavors to our city. Despite initial resistance from our parents, we launched the venture with secret family recipes and a lot of passion. Our food quickly gained popularity, and even our parents began to support us. Tandoori Bites became a hub of laughter and delicious food, uniting people through our bold and authentic flavors. As we laughed, cooked, and served our way into the hearts of food enthusiasts, we realized that sometimes, the best way to make a difference is to follow your passion, even if it means defying the doubts of those closest to you
